Justins noodleriffic rendition of the fast food noodle bowl

This dish was inspired by my wifes love of 'Cup-Bop', a Korean style noodle bowl chain. The delicious noodles and tasty toppings are enough to make anyone fall in love. So I've adapted the idea by korean frying chicken and topping garlic chili noodles with the crispy morsels.

Ingredients:

  • Trader Joe's Curly Noodles
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Scallion
  • Chilies or Chili flakes
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Corn Starch
  • Soy Sauce

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a small pot. Mince the aeromatics and place them in a bowl with the chilies. pour over the hot oil to make chili oil! Boil the noodles and toss them with the deliciousness

  2. Combine flour and cornstarch in a bowl. placew marinated chicken in the mixture and toss to coat. deep fry until golden and crispy

  3. Arrange bowls with noodles and chicken on top, and add kimchi, cucumber, or any other toppings you'd like!
