Justins simplistic how to grill chicken

This dish is a overly simplified interpretation of how to grill a chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Marinade
  • Grill

Instructions:

  1. Marinade chicken in anything you want

  2. Preheat grill to somewhere between 15 and 600 degrees farenheight

  3. Place chicken on grill, and dont remove until fully cooked. Serve with my delicious IceWater recipe!
